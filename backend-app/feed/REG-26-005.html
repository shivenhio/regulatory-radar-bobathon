ECHA · REACH · SVHC Candidate List update · Published 2026-05-02

REACH SVHC list adds 1,4-dioxane

Reference ID: REG-26-005 · Type: new
Effective / deadline: 2026-05-02 · Severity: low
Scope - categories: all
Scope - substances: dioxane · Markets: EU
Conditions: Applies to articles containing 1,4-dioxane > 0.1% w/w (none expected in this portfolio).

1,4-Dioxane is added to the SVHC Candidate List. Producers of articles containing it above 0.1% w/w must notify and inform recipients.

Action required: Confirm absence/presence of 1,4-dioxane in articles.
