Stricter chemical limits for toys (phthalates, bisphenols)

Reference ID: REG-26-024 · Type: amendment
Effective / deadline: 2027-09-01 · Severity: high
Scope - categories: toy_electronic
Scope - substances: n/a · Markets: EU
Conditions: Applies to electronic toys intended for children (not 14+ STEM/maker products).

The revised toy-safety rules tighten limits on phthalates and bisphenols in toys intended for children. Products designed for users 14+ are not 'toys' for this purpose.

Action required: Re-test toy materials against the tighter chemical limits.
