France: repairability/durability index for displays, appliances and networking gear

Reference ID: REG-26-043 · Type: new
Effective / deadline: 2026-09-15 · Severity: medium
Scope - categories: display, appliance, networking
Scope - substances: n/a · Markets: FR
Conditions: Applies to displays, household appliances and networking equipment sold in France (FR).

Products sold in France in the covered categories must display a repairability (moving to durability) index score at the point of sale.

Action required: Calculate and display the French repairability/durability index.
