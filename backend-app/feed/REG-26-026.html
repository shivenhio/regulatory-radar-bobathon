MDR guidance: wellness vs. medical wearables

Reference ID: REG-26-026 · Type: new
Effective / deadline: 2026-10-01 · Severity: high
Scope - categories: medical_wearable, wearable
Scope - substances: n/a · Markets: EU
Conditions: Applies to body-worn devices with a medical intended purpose.

New guidance clarifies when a body-worn device has a medical purpose and therefore falls under MDR (CE under MDR, clinical evidence). General fitness/activity trackers without a medical claim remain consumer products under GPSR.

Action required: Classify body-worn devices; route medical-purpose devices to MDR.
