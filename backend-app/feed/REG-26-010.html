EUR-Lex · Battery · Regulation (EU) 2023/1542, Art. 77 (battery passport) · Published 2026-04-18

Battery passport required for LMT and industrial (>2 kWh) batteries

Reference ID: REG-26-010 · Type: new
Effective / deadline: 2027-02-18 · Severity: high
Scope - categories: battery_pack, emobility_battery
Scope - substances: n/a · Markets: EU
Conditions: Applies to LMT batteries and industrial batteries; NOT to portable consumer batteries.

From the application date, a digital battery passport is mandatory for light-means-of-transport (LMT) batteries and industrial batteries above 2 kWh, as well as EV batteries. Portable consumer batteries are out of scope of the passport requirement.

Action required: Stand up a battery passport (QR + data carrier) for in-scope batteries.
