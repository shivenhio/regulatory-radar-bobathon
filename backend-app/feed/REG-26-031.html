EUR-Lex · FoodContact · Regulation (EU) 2024/3190 · Published 2026-03-19

Food-contact materials: BPA ban in food packaging and utensils

Reference ID: REG-26-031 · Type: new
Effective / deadline: 2026-12-20 · Severity: medium
Scope - categories: food-contact materials & articles
Scope - substances: BPA · Markets: EU
Conditions: Restriction applies to materials and articles intended for food contact; it does not regulate BPA used in electronics enclosures.

Bisphenol A is banned in materials and articles intended to come into contact with food (e.g. can coatings, reusable bottles, kitchenware).

Action required: Relevant only to food-contact materials and articles.
