EUR-Lex · CBAM · Regulation (EU) 2023/956 · Published 2026-01-30

CBAM definitive regime reporting for importers of metals/cement

Reference ID: REG-26-028 · Type: new
Effective / deadline: 2026-01-01 · Severity: low
Scope - categories: raw aluminium / steel / cement / fertilisers (not finished EEE)
Scope - substances: n/a · Markets: EU
Conditions: Covers importers of listed raw commodities, not finished electronics.

Importers of aluminium, iron, steel, cement, fertilisers, hydrogen and electricity must purchase CBAM certificates and report embedded emissions.

Action required: Relevant only to importers of covered raw commodities.
