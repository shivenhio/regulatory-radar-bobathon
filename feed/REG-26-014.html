EUR-Lex · RED · Directive 2014/53/EU, Art. 3(4) (common charger) · Published 2026-01-12

USB-C common charger mandate for portable electronics

Reference ID: REG-26-014 · Type: amendment
Effective / deadline: 2024-12-28 · Severity: high
Scope - categories: audio, wearable, smart_home, drone
Scope - substances: n/a · Markets: EU
Conditions: Applies to portable electronics with a wired charging port (USB-C/micro-USB/proprietary).

Portable consumer electronics that recharge via a wired cable must use a USB-C receptacle. Devices still shipping with micro-USB or proprietary connectors must transition. The obligation applies at the point of placing on the EU market.

Action required: Switch wired-charge ports to USB-C across in-scope SKUs.
