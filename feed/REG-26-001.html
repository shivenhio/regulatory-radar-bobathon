EUR-Lex · RoHS · Directive 2011/65/EU, Annex II review · Published 2026-05-04

RoHS heavy-metal limits review (Pb, Hg, Cd, Cr VI)

Reference ID: REG-26-001 · Type: amendment
Effective / deadline: 2027-07-22 · Severity: high
Scope - categories: all
Scope - substances: lead, mercury, cadmium, hexavalent_chromium · Markets: EU
Conditions: Applies to EEE placed on the EU market containing the listed heavy metals above threshold.

The Commission has reopened the Annex II review for the four restricted heavy metals. Several lighting and legacy-electronics exemptions for lead and mercury are set to lapse, meaning equipment containing these metals above 0.1% (0.01% for cadmium) must be reformulated or pulled from the EU market.

Action required: Confirm homogeneous-material content of Pb/Hg/Cd/Cr VI; re-test affected SKUs.
